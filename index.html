Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our algorithmic trading platform

What is algo trading?

Algo trading uses automated systems to execute trades based on pre‑defined strategies, removing human emotions and enhancing efficiency. Our algorithms analyze market conditions 24/7 and make trading decisions based on statistical patterns and technical indicators.

How do I start using House of Algo's?

Simply sign up, install MT4, and connect to our strategies. Our system will handle the trades automatically. Our team will guide you through the entire setup process to ensure a smooth onboarding experience.

Do I need trading experience?

No, our automated strategies are designed for both beginners and experienced traders. The algorithms handle all trading decisions, so you don't need prior trading knowledge to benefit from our platform.

What is the minimum investment required?

You can invest a minimum of $100. For higher investments, multiples of $1000 apply (e.g., $2000, $4000, etc.). Different investment levels may access different strategy tiers with varying risk-reward profiles.

What are the expected returns and risks?

Returns are 2% to 5% monthly (after a 30% company share). The highest drawdown so far is 30% (53% in extreme backtesting conditions). Remember that all trading involves risk, and past performance is not indicative of future results.

Is there a locking period?

No, you can stop copy trading anytime and withdraw your funds immediately. We believe in providing full flexibility to our clients, with no lock-in periods or hidden withdrawal restrictions.

What is the account opening procedure?

A link will be sent to you. By clicking it, you must fill in all the details (Aadhar Card, Driving License, Bank Statement, Email ID, Mobile Number). The account will be opened in 24 hours with support from our team.

Still have questions? Contact our support team for assistance.
